About

The word of God is living, and what matters most is what comes to live within us.

Living Words Music is a home for songs written in worship of God. The songs move through the seasons of faith. Some are made to lift a room in praise. Others are made to sit quietly with God in the lonely places, where words run thin. Each one begins as words on a page and is offered, plainly, to whoever might need it.

Behind the songs is James Thompson, a writer who hopes only that they find people where they are, and point past themselves to King Jesus.

Churches and worship leaders are warmly welcome to use these songs. Lyrics and chord charts for each song are free to download from its page.
